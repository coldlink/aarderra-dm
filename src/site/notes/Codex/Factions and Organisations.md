# Main Organisations
## Eitra

The formal name of individual global guilds.

Independent guilds are virtually extinct, having been systematically bought out by the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Florini\|#Florini]].

### The Eitra of Drapers

A specialised guild branch responsible for cloth production, textile weaving, and the garment trade within [[Codex/Places and Navigation#Terra Calamarea\|Terra Calamarea]], primarily based in the town of [[Codex/Places and Navigation#Dijendam\|Dijendam]].

Once an independent collective of traditional weavers, it now operates as a unique joint-chartered entity split among all [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Florini\|three great Florini families]].

The guild serves as the industrial link balancing [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Van Raccolto family\|Van Raccolto's]] raw fibre supply, [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Orolumen family\|Orolumen's]] clockwork loom technology, and [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Salvaren family\|Salvaren's]] exclusive distribution of the finished cloth bolts.

## Florini

The Florini are the three elite, ultra-wealthy merchant dynasties (or great houses) that rule the global economy of Aarderra as massive corporate syndicates.

They systematically absorbed nearly all independent trade guilds into their pipelines, creating an absolute monopoly on the world's resources, manufacturing, and commoditised magic.

The primary three families being the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Van Raccolto family\|#Van Raccolto family]], the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Salvaren family\|#Salvaren family]], and the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Orolumen family\|#Orolumen family]].

### Van Raccolto family
*Mining, Raw Materials & Refinement*

House Van Raccolto specialise entirely in deep earth mining, heavy resource harvesting, and the primary refinement of the elements before they are shipped to the capital.

### Salvaren family
*Distribution & Logistics*

The Salvarens maintain absolute authority over the physical movement of goods, managing the vast transport networks, shipping ports, and high-speed trade corridors that span the world.

### Orolumen family
*Artisanal & Manufacturing*

The Orolumen syndicate hold a monopoly on artisanal craftsmanship, taking refined metals, fibres, and components and transforming them into arcane machinery, textiles, and practical consumer goods.

## Molisyni

The global mercantile church, worshipping the God of Justice, [[Codex/Gods#Forestii\|Forestii]]. The central banking empire, and primary custodian of civil society.

Driven by a philosophy of divine stewardship and balanced ledger books, the Molisyni manages the grand financial lines of the world.
