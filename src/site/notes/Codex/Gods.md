## Forestii
*The God of Justice*

Forestii is the god of justice, law, and truth. His faith is rooted in the ancient folklore of a benevolent peacemaker who sat within a radiant hall of gold and silver pillars, revered as an arbiter who could resolve any conflict so perfectly that all parties departed in mutual satisfaction.

The [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Molisyni\|Molysini]] models its grand cathedrals on this legendary hall, teaching that true justice relies on objective, structured balance. This is a concept symbolised by Forestii's sacred scales.

## Saints

Under the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Molisyni\|Molysini]], [[Codex/Gods#Forestii\|Forestii]] stands alone as the supreme deity and ultimate High Judge, with all other traditional figures integrated into the faith as ***Canonised Saints***.

Each of the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Florini\|Florini]] families also maintain an exclusive patron saint to watch over their household and safeguard their specific commercial interests.

While the official church endorses these sanctioned figures to reinforce civic order, some common people frequently turn to unendorsed ***Folk Saints*** for comfort. For the church this is a minor administrative violation that local parish priests typically overlook, provided the weekly tithing boxes remain full.
