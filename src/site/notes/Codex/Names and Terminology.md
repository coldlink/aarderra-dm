## [[Codex/Places and Navigation\|Geography and Major Locations]]

### [[Codex/Places and Navigation#Aarderra\|Aarderra]] (The Aar)

The name of the world globe itself.

### [[Codex/Places and Navigation#Terra Calamarea\|Terra Calamarea]]

The vast expanse of civilised land directly governed and taxed by the capital city of Calamarea, encompassing the vast majority of the known world.

### [[Codex/Places and Navigation#Calamarea\|Calamarea]]

The sprawling marble heart of the world, its capital metropolis, and the literal magnetic north pole of Aarderra.

## [[Codex/Factions and Organisations\|Factions and Organisations]]

### Eitra

The formal name of individual global guilds. Independent guilds are virtually extinct, having been systematically bought out by the [[Codex/Names and Terminology#Florini\|#Florini]].

### Florini

The ultra-wealthy, elite, merchant families that sit at the absolute top of the Calmarean hierarchy.

The primary three families being the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Van Raccolto family\|Van Raccolto]], the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Salvaren family\|Salvaren]], and the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#Orolumen family\|Orolumen]].

### Molisyni

The global mercantile church, worshipping the God of Justice, Forestii. The central banking empire, and primary custodian of civil society. Driven by a philosophy of divine stewardship and balanced ledger books, the Molisyni manages the grand financial lines of the world.
