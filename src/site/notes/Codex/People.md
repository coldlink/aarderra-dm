#### High Warden Ludovico Sartori
*Human - he/him*

The chief authority of the [[Codex/Places and Navigation#The Dijendam Entrepôts\|Dijendam Entrepôts]] and a prominent leader within the [[Codex/Factions and Organisations#The Eitra of Drapers\|Eitra of Drapers]] who champions clockwork textile automation.

He is the host of an exclusive, invite-only gala at his private estate, [[Codex/Places and Navigation#Castle Marblyash\|Castle Marblyash]], celebrating the guild's newest industrial breakthrough.
