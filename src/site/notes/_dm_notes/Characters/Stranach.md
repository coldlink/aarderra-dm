Stranach's painful break-up with Ludovico pushed her to leave Aarderra and follow her childhood dream of seeing the world, and she set off on a sailing voyage, over time becoming a skilled and well-respected navigator. Returning to Aarderra after all these years, she heard about Ludovico's party and conspired to get in through tagging along with a mutual friend, hoping to show off all she's accomplished since they last met.
