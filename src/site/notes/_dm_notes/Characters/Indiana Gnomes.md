I became an archaeologist after I enjoyed digging up treasures in gardens when I was young. I decided to turn this passion into a career and travelled the world uncovering items. In recent years I’ve put my energy into thieving back archeological artefacts that have been wrongly stolen and returning them to their rightful place.
