Artemisia is the only adopted daughter of a famed and wealthy jewel-smith, and a high ranking cleric in the city. If she’d been left to her original path in life, she’d have likely ended up a begger or a thief on the streets, if she’d even lived that long outside the orphanage to find out. Lucky for her, she instead rose to become the beloved (and somewhat indulged) daughter to a house that granted her access to money, social standing and extravagance — all of which she has grown deeply fond of. She is accustomed to finery, has a weakness for embroidered frock-coats, but she also remembers well how close she came to a life of complete poverty, and is adamant to not let herself or her family end up in that state.
