I was born a member of minor nobility, but didn’t fit in. After a horse riding accident as a youth, a strange mark upon my face prevented my debut in high society and landed me in a university. I found a vocation in the study of divination and since then I’ve attempted to use my skills to enter the employ of powerful people. I’ve worked for a few unsavoury characters and employed a level of moral flexibility, but I’m sure there’s a line I wouldn’t cross… somewhere.
