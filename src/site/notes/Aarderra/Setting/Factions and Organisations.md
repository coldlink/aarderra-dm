# Main Organisations
## Eitra

The formal name of individual global guilds.

Independent guilds are virtually extinct, having been systematically bought out by the [[Aarderra/Setting/Factions and Organisations#Florini\|#Florini]].

### The Eitra of Drapers
## Florini

The ultra-wealthy, elite, merchant families that sit at the absolute top of the Calmarean hierarchy.

The primary three families being the Van Raccolto, the Salvaren, and the Orolumen.

## Molisyni

The global mercantile church, worshipping the God of Justice, Forestii. The central banking empire, and primary custodian of civil society.

Driven by a philosophy of divine stewardship and balanced ledger books, the Molisyni manages the grand financial lines of the world.
