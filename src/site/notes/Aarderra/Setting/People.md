#### High Warden Ludovico Sartori
*Human - he/him*

The chief authority of the [[Aarderra/Setting/Places and Navigation#The Dijendam Entrepôts\|Dijendam Entrepôts]] and a prominent leader within the [[Aarderra/Setting/Factions and Organisations#The Eitra of Drapers\|Eitra of Drapers]] who champions clockwork textile automation.

He is the host of the exclusive, invite-only gala celebrating the town's newest industrial breakthrough.
